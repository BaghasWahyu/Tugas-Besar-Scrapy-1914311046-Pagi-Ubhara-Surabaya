Chapter 194: Chapter 194

As she looked down at that face, bitter sourness filled Feng Zhiwei’s heart.

She bit down on her lips, stifling the sudden emotion and smothering it down deep.

Ning Yi turned to her and smiled, waving her over as he called out: “You’re here.”

“En.”

A casual call and a simple answer as if meeting back at the Xi Yard after finishing her day, calmly greeted under the whisky clouds as a gentle breeze weaved around them.

As if all that danger was distant, as if those thousands of hostile enemies had never existed.

“Come.” Ning Yi called again.

Feng Zhiwei walked over slowly, stopping a distance away.

Ning Yi heard her steps stop and he furrowed his brows as he smiled: “Why so bashful today? Were you scared?”

Feng Zhiwei smiled back but did not move forward. “How are things inside?”

“The same.” Ning Yi replied, standing and sweeping aside his board. He reached out to pull her close: “Do you have food? I haven’t eaten the whole day and I’m starving.”

Feng Zhiwei dodged his hand and stepped back, replying: “No.”

“What is with you?” Ning Yi frowned again, stopping. “Are you blaming me for not grabbing them? Ancestral Temple in South Sea cannot be disturbed, and if I had moved in it would only be bad for you in the future, so I decided to wait…”

“No, it’s not that. “Feng Zhiwei interrupted. “You can’t grabbing them with force; if it were me, I would only have been able to do this as well.”

“Perhaps.” Ning Yi smiled coldly. “This Prince’s patience has its limit. If the Yan Family truly dares to not give face to the Imperial Court, this Prince also dares to leave them no retreat.”

He walked up to Feng Zhiwei and she stepped back. Just as he reached for her sleeve, she shifted away; as they neared, her fragrance drifted by his nose and he smelled something a little different. Ning Yi paused, but when he breathed again, she had already moved away.

He stood quietly, his face slowly smoothing out. He did not comment on his thoughts, only saying coldly: “Since you are here and this is your business, I will not involve myself. You can make the decisions.”

Feng Zhiwei watched quietly as he turned away and gathered up his guards and prepared them to depart.

The pitter patter of footfalls rushed over, and Feng Zhiwei turned to take in a dainty, cute young woman dressed in coarse clothing. As she reached the tree barricade, she tied up her cotton dress around her waist as she moved to climb over. A guard immediately blocked her way with his spear and called out: “Identify yourself!”

“South Sea Feng Zhou City’s Qian Shui[1] Village, Hua Qiong begs to speak to His Highness.” The woman announced clearly, her eyes large and bright and her brows exquisite on her slightly dark face.

Ning Yi turned.

The young woman kowtowed on the tree and called out: “Your Highness, this commoner can open the gates!”

Feng Zhiwei and Ning Yi turned and met each others gaze, happiness in their eyes — only a direct Yan Family member could open the gates of the Ancestral Temple. Right now, no one would dare open the gates for Feng Zhiwei and Ning Yi, but if someone came who was willing and able to open the way, the problem would disappear.

“Who are you?” Ning Yi called out calmly. “Your surname is Hua, not Yan. If you are not part of the Yan Family, opening the gates is a capital crime. Do not court death.”

“Your Highness.” Hua Qiong kowtowed again, calling out clearly: “My Mother-in-Law and husband are inside the Ancestral Temple. If I must live without them, I would rather die!”

The prince and Feng Zhiwei exchanged looks of shock: “Husband?”

Feng Zhiwei was startled; she had never imagined that Yan Huaishi would have a wife in South Sea. Why had he never mentioned it? And why was his wife, a member of the Yan Family, wearing the clothes of a fisherwoman?

Feng Zhiwei’s eyes drifted down to her hands and feet. The woman wore straw sandals on bare feet and her inner pants were rolled up high. Bloody rope marks could still be seen on her wrists and ankles, and Feng Zhiwei could even make out bone.

How had she come here? Escaping bonds and running over? Was that why her sandals were broken and her body covered in wounds?

“Let her through.” Feng Zhiwei called.

The guard stepped aside and Hua Qiong clambered laboriously over the tree, ignoring Ning Yi and Feng Zhiwei as she rushed to the Ancestral Temple’s doors.

As she ran, she unhooked a pair of harpoons from slings around her back.

Feng Zhiwei grunted in surprise, watching in stunned amazement.

What was going on?

Feng Zhiwei followed worriedly as Hua Qiong began knocking on the door, yelling: “The Eldest Grandson from the Main Branch[2], Yan Changtian,[3] scion of the Yan Family’s 732nd Generation begs to see the Yan Family Head!”

Feng Zhiwei and Ning Yi turned towards each other again, both of them trying to remember if they had heard this name over the past days. The Eldest Grandson?

Yan Chantian was clearly a man’s name, and this woman called herself Hua Qiong?

The door cracked open a sliver and a face peered out. Yan Huaiyuan turned his pale face to Ning Yi and Feng Zhiwei before looking at Hua Qiong. Surprise flashed over his face before he began swearing.

“You little widow! Bitch! What Yan Changtian? Who is Yan Changtian? The Yan Family only has 731 generations! Where did this 732 come from? You dare come here with a different surname and knock on our Ancestral Temple’s doors? How dare you blaspheme before our temple, on this sacred land! I’ll kill you!”

“Kill me if you dare!” Hua Qiong announced bravely.”If you dare offend your ancestors and overturn the rites of your family, killing your Yan Family’s Main Branch’s Eldest Grandson, I won’t stop you!”

“What Eldest Grandson? Piss off!” Yan Huaiyuan roared furiously, extending his hand to push the young woman.

That was when Hua Qiong stepped back and shouted, boldly lifting up her shirt to reveal her belly: “Yan Changtian is here!”

The thousand onlookers fell silent.

Feng Zhiwei’s mouth fell open.

Gu Nanyi stared blankly at that prominent belly, his eyes turning from that pregnant flesh to the walnut in his hand.

Ning Cheng fell to the ground, stirring up a cloud of dust.

The young woman lifted up her shirt in the bright daylight, revealing her waist to all the onlookers around her. Her protruding belly was only covered by a thin, sheer undergarment that could not even hide the stretch marks.

Yan Huaiyuan stilled in stunned amazement, his extended arm forgotten.

“Your Yan Family’s 732nd Eldest Branch Eldest Grandson is now in my womb.” Hua Qiong called out, her eyes bright. She stood unembarrassed and unafraid, spine upright under Yan Huiayuan’s gaze. “According to the Yan Family’s genealogy, the 732nd generation should be named ‘Chang,’ so I named my son Changtian. Yan Huaiyuan, right now, Yan Changtian needs to enter!”

[1] Thousand Water

[2] The age of “eldest of the main branch” does not compete with grand children of the side branches.

[3] Long Sky

Bab 194: Bab 194 Saat dia menatap wajah itu, rasa asam pahit memenuhi hati Feng Zhiwei.

Dia menggigit bibirnya, menahan emosi yang tiba-tiba dan menahannya dalam-dalam.

Ning Yi menoleh padanya dan tersenyum, melambai padanya saat dia berseru: “Kamu di sini.”

“En.”

Panggilan biasa dan jawaban sederhana seolah bertemu kembali di Xi Yard setelah menyelesaikan harinya, dengan tenang disambut di bawah awan wiski saat angin sepoi-sepoi bertiup di sekitar mereka.

Seolah-olah semua bahaya itu jauh, seolah ribuan musuh yang bermusuhan itu tidak pernah ada.

“Datang.” Ning Yi menelepon lagi.

Feng Zhiwei berjalan perlahan, berhenti agak jauh.

Ning Yi mendengar langkahnya berhenti dan dia mengerutkan alisnya saat dia tersenyum: “Mengapa begitu malu hari ini? Apakah kamu takut? ”

Feng Zhiwei balas tersenyum tetapi tidak bergerak maju. “Bagaimana hal-hal di dalamnya?”

“Sama.” Ning Yi menjawab, berdiri dan menyapu papannya. Dia mengulurkan tangan untuk menariknya mendekat: “Apakah kamu punya makanan? Aku belum makan sepanjang hari dan Aku kelaparan. ”

Feng Zhiwei menghindari tangannya dan melangkah mundur, menjawab: “Tidak.”

“Ada apa denganmu?” Ning Yi mengerutkan kening lagi, berhenti. “Apakah Kamu menyalahkan Aku karena tidak menangkap mereka? Kuil Leluhur di Laut Selatan tidak dapat diganggu, dan jika Aku pindah ke dalamnya hanya akan berdampak buruk bagi Kamu di masa depan, jadi Aku memutuskan untuk menunggu… ”

“Tidak, bukan itu. “Feng Zhiwei menyela. “Kamu tidak bisa menangkap mereka dengan paksa; jika itu aku, aku hanya akan bisa melakukan ini juga. ”

“Mungkin.” Ning Yi tersenyum dingin. Kesabaran Pangeran ini ada batasnya. Jika Keluarga Yan benar-benar berani untuk tidak menghadap ke Istana Kekaisaran, Pangeran ini juga berani membiarkan mereka tidak mundur. ”

Dia berjalan ke arah Feng Zhiwei dan dia melangkah mundur. Saat dia meraih lengan bajunya, dia bergeser menjauh; ketika mereka mendekat, aromanya melayang di hidungnya dan dia mencium sesuatu yang sedikit berbeda. Ning Yi berhenti, tetapi ketika dia bernapas lagi, dia sudah pindah.

Dia berdiri dengan tenang, wajahnya perlahan merapikan. Dia tidak mengomentari pikirannya, hanya berkata dengan dingin: “Karena kamu ada di sini dan ini urusanmu, Aku tidak akan melibatkan diri. Kamu bisa membuat keputusan. ”

Feng Zhiwei memperhatikan dengan tenang saat dia berbalik dan mengumpulkan pengawalnya dan mempersiapkan mereka untuk pergi.

Derai langkah kaki yang berdesir bergegas, dan Feng Zhiwei menoleh untuk melihat seorang wanita muda cantik dan cantik yang mengenakan pakaian kasar. Saat dia mencapai barikade pohon, dia mengikatkan gaun katunnya di pinggangnya saat dia bergerak untuk memanjat. Seorang penjaga segera memblokir jalannya dengan tombaknya dan berseru: “Identifikasi dirimu!”

Desa Qian Shui [1] Kota Feng Zhou di Laut Selatan, Hua Qiong memohon untuk berbicara dengan Yang Mulia. ” Wanita itu mengumumkan dengan jelas, matanya besar dan cerah dan alisnya indah di wajahnya yang agak gelap.

Ning Yi berbalik.

Wanita muda itu bersujud di pohon dan berseru: “Yang Mulia, orang biasa ini dapat membuka gerbangnya!”

Feng Zhiwei dan Ning Yi berbalik dan bertemu satu sama lain, kebahagiaan di mata mereka – hanya anggota Keluarga Yan langsung yang bisa membuka gerbang Kuil Leluhur. Saat ini, tidak ada yang berani membuka gerbang untuk Feng Zhiwei dan Ning Yi, tetapi jika seseorang datang yang bersedia dan mampu membuka jalan, masalahnya akan hilang.

“Kamu siapa?” Ning Yi memanggil dengan tenang. “Nama belakangmu adalah Hua, bukan Yan. Jika Kamu bukan bagian dari Keluarga Yan, membuka gerbang adalah kejahatan besar. Jangan mengadili kematian. ”

“Yang mulia.” Hua Qiong bersujud lagi, berteriak dengan jelas: “Ibu mertuaku dan suamiku ada di dalam Kuil Leluhur. Jika Aku harus hidup tanpa mereka, Aku lebih baik mati! ”

Pangeran dan Feng Zhiwei saling bertukar pandangan kaget: “Suami?”

Feng Zhiwei terkejut; dia tidak pernah membayangkan bahwa Yan Huaishi akan memiliki istri di Laut Selatan. Mengapa dia tidak pernah menyebutkannya? Dan mengapa istrinya, anggota Keluarga Yan, mengenakan pakaian wanita nelayan?

Mata Feng Zhiwei mengarah ke tangan dan kakinya. Wanita itu mengenakan sandal jerami dengan kaki telanjang dan celana dalamnya digulung tinggi. Tanda tali berdarah masih bisa dilihat di pergelangan tangan dan pergelangan kakinya, dan Feng Zhiwei bahkan bisa melihat tulang.

Bagaimana haapakah dia datang ke sini? Melarikan diri dari ikatan dan lari? Itukah sebabnya sandalnya patah dan tubuhnya penuh luka?

Biarkan dia lewat. Feng Zhiwei menelepon.

Penjaga itu menyingkir dan Hua Qiong dengan susah payah memanjat pohon, mengabaikan Ning Yi dan Feng Zhiwei saat dia bergegas ke pintu Kuil Leluhur.

Saat dia berlari, dia melepaskan sepasang tombak dari gendongan di punggungnya.

Feng Zhiwei mendengus kaget, menyaksikan dengan takjub.

Apa yang sedang terjadi?

Feng Zhiwei mengikuti dengan cemas saat Hua Qiong mulai mengetuk pintu, berteriak: “Cucu Tertua dari Cabang Utama [2], Yan Changtian, [3] keturunan dari Generasi ke-732 Keluarga Yan memohon untuk bertemu dengan Kepala Keluarga Yan!”

Feng Zhiwei dan Ning Yi saling berpaling lagi, keduanya mencoba mengingat apakah mereka pernah mendengar nama ini selama beberapa hari terakhir. Cucu Tertua?

Yan Chantian jelas merupakan nama laki-laki, dan wanita ini menyebut dirinya Hua Qiong?

Pintunya terbuka sedikit dan sebuah wajah mengintip ke luar. Yan Huaiyuan memalingkan wajah pucatnya ke Ning Yi dan Feng Zhiwei sebelum melihat Hua Qiong. Kejutan melintas di wajahnya sebelum dia mulai mengumpat.

“Kamu janda kecil! Menggerutu! Apa Yan Changtian? Siapa Yan Changtian? Keluarga Yan hanya memiliki 731 generasi! Dari mana datangnya 732 ini? Kamu berani datang ke sini dengan nama keluarga yang berbeda dan mengetuk pintu Kuil Leluhur kita? Beraninya Kamu menghujat di depan kuil kami, di tanah suci ini! Aku akan membunuhmu!”

“Bunuh aku jika kamu berani!” Hua Qiong mengumumkan dengan berani. “Jika Kamu berani menyinggung leluhur Kamu dan membatalkan ritus keluarga Kamu, membunuh Cucu Tertua Cabang Utama Keluarga Yan Kamu, Aku tidak akan menghentikan Kamu!”

“Apa Cucu Tertua? Kesal!” Yan Huaiyuan meraung marah, mengulurkan tangannya untuk mendorong wanita muda itu.

Saat itulah Hua Qiong melangkah mundur dan berteriak, dengan berani mengangkat kemejanya untuk memperlihatkan perutnya: “Yan Changtian ada di sini!”

Ribuan penonton terdiam.

Mulut Feng Zhiwei ternganga.

Gu Nanyi menatap kosong ke perut yang menonjol itu, matanya beralih dari daging hamil ke kenari di tangannya.

Ning Cheng jatuh ke tanah, menimbulkan awan debu.

Wanita muda itu mengangkat bajunya di siang hari yang cerah, memperlihatkan pinggangnya kepada semua penonton di sekitarnya. Perutnya yang buncit hanya ditutupi oleh pakaian dalam yang tipis dan tipis yang bahkan tidak bisa menyembunyikan stretch mark.

Yan Huaiyuan terdiam dengan takjub, lengannya yang terulur terlupakan.

“Cucu Tertua Cabang Tertua ke-732 Keluarga Yan Kamu sekarang ada di dalam rahim Aku.” Hua Qiong berseru, matanya cerah. Dia berdiri tanpa rasa malu dan tidak takut, punggung tegak di bawah tatapan Yan Huiayuan. “Menurut silsilah Keluarga Yan, generasi ke-732 harus diberi nama ‘Chang,’ jadi Aku menamai putra Aku Changtian. Yan Huaiyuan, sekarang, Yan Changtian harus masuk! ”

[1] Seribu Air

[2] Usia “sulung dari cabang utama” tidak bersaing dengan cucu dari cabang samping.

[3] Langit Panjang
