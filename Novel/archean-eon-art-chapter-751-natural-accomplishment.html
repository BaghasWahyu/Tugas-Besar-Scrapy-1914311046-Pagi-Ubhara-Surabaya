Chapter 751: Natural Accomplishment

Translator: CKtalon 

In Heavenly Dream Realm, the Heaven Connection Tree connected the sky and the ground. An ordinary leaf was dozens of kilometers wide. The Heaven Connection Tree’s crown spanned more than 50,000 kilometers, and there were stretches of buildings across it. It was where the entire Heavenly Dream Realm’s divine court was located.

In a hidden hall in Heavenly Dream Realm’s divine court.

A white-haired elder sat cross-legged. Beside him was an old woman, who was waiting for him silently.

The white-haired elder’s power surged into an ancient array formation hidden in the hall. Through the array formation, invisible waves spread across the River of Spacetime.

Let the world enter my dream. The white-haired elder’s consciousness entered a dream world, and the dream world reflected the entire River of Spacetime.

The white-haired elder looked at the various places. Apart from Eternal Tower, Black Fiend Hall, Dragon Ancestral Land, high-level inhabited worlds, and a few areas where Eighth Tribulation Eminences had set up array formations, he could see everything else. Even White Avian Hall and other places couldn’t stop him.

City Lord Eastcalm, Hall Lord White Avian. The white-haired elder naturally probed the two strongest existences in the River of Spacetime. In the illusory dream world, no other living beings could sense his spying. Meng Chuan and Hall Lord White Avian could sense it, but it was difficult for them to know where the spying came from.

How can two half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminences withstand the Progenitor’s means?

His progenitor was an Eighth Tribulation Eminence. He was skilled in dreams and was extremely good at spying.

With a dream, he could dream of everything in the River of Spacetime. It was impossible to guard against.

The white-haired elder was a Seventh Tribulation deity. He used the array formation left behind by his progenitor to reflect the entire River of Spacetime into his dream.

Whoosh.

The white-haired elder opened his eyes after stopping the array formation.

“Your Majesty,” said the old woman.

“I used the Progenitor’s array formation to observe the various areas in the River of Spacetime. Compared to 300 years ago, there aren’t many changes,” said the white-haired elder. “The strongest Hall Lord White Avian and City Lord Eastcalm are still at the half-step Eighth Tribulation realm only.”

Although high-level worlds had little contact with the outside world, they often observed changes in the outside world.

The old woman nodded slightly. She wasn’t a deity; she was the strongest expert in Heavenly Dream Realm’s current era, a Sixth Tribulation Eminence. After cultivating to such a realm… the next time the Progenitor woke up, she would be bestowed a seat of divinity when she died. From then on, she would live as long as the heavens.

The white-haired elder was a Seventh Tribulation Godfiend. He was the strongest in Heavenly Dream Realm’s history apart from the Progenitor. Only with the strength of a Seventh Tribulation Eminence could he better cast the many array formations left behind by the Progenitor.

Eighth Tribulation Eminences had to cross one time period after another so that they could maintain their youth. These deities had been alive for a long time. Even though they relied on sleeping and reincarnation methods, their consciousness remained distorted.

Over time, they would become different. They would gradually be assimilated by their divinity; this was something that couldn’t be helped. Without sufficient willpower, they wouldn’t be able to maintain themselves even if they had a long lifespan.

“By the way, look at the traces of time on City Lord Eastcalm,” said the white-haired elder with a smile. “He has cultivated for more than 33,000 years. Compared to last time… City Lord Eastcalm definitely has an Essence Soul avatar that’s located in a place where time flows 33-fold faster.”

“He’s a half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminence. A place that can maintain a time flow 33-fold faster than normal? How terrifying is the energy expenditure?” The old woman was alarmed. “I’ve never heard of such a place.”

“Therefore, he must’ve encountered a special opportunity. He might’ve gone beyond the universe,” said the white-haired elder.

It was very normal to go outside the universe.

Hall Lord White Avian, Celestial Thearch Myriad Star, and Guild Master Fiend Eye had similar opportunities in the past. They had obtained the favor of an Eighth Tribulation Eminence. If they were willing to bring them out, they could naturally roam beyond the universe.

“Your Majesty, when do you plan on entering your slumber?” asked the old woman.

“In this era, two half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminences are still alive. I won’t sleep for the time being; I’ll sleep after they die of old age,” said the white-haired elder.

“According to the 33-fold faster time flow, City Lord Eastcalm will reach the end of his lifespan in 5,000 years. We will see his cultivation outcome,” said the old woman with a smile.

“He won’t succeed.” The white-haired elder shook his head. “Progenitor said that reaching the Eighth Tribulation realm is extremely difficult. The Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation realm… is even harder to reach than the Physical Body Eighth Tribulation realm.”

The old woman nodded slightly and said, “By the way, Your Majesty, my disciple—Meng Hu—was once good friends with City Lord Eastcalm. They cleared Fiend Mountain together.”

“How’s his experience in Hundred Lives Dream Realm?” asked the white-haired elder. As a Fifth Tribulation Eminence in Heavenly Dream Realm, Meng Hu was similarly given close attention. After all, it was already not bad for a Sixth Tribulation Eminence to appear in a high-level world. Many times, there were no Sixth Tribulation Eminences.

“He has experienced more than half of it, but he still hasn’t sunk into it,” said the old woman worriedly. “However, the deeper one goes into Hundred Lives Dream Realm, the more dangerous it becomes.”

“If he survives, he will benefit from a disaster. He will also reach the Sixth Tribulation realm in his life,” said the white-haired elder. “If he fails, it means that he doesn’t have the required bearing.”

…

In the outer realm, White Avian Hall, Library.

Meng Chuan was reading a book.

Huh? Meng Chuan slightly frowned when he vaguely sensed someone spying on him. Which high-level faction is spying on me? 

After Meng Chuan became a half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminence, he learned that factions from high-level worlds would occasionally spy on various places in the River of Spacetime. Before he grasped the laws of spacetime, he didn’t notice them. Now that he had discovered them… he didn’t know which faction was spying on him. After all, there were dozens of factions in the River of Spacetime.

Every such faction had an Eighth Tribulation Eminence backing them.

Soon, the spying sensation disappeared.

“Meng Chuan.” Hall Lord White Avian came to the library.

“Hall Lord, you sensed it too?” Meng Chuan looked at Hall Lord White Avian.

“Yes.” Hall Lord White Avian nodded. “However, don’t worry about it. They can only hide in their lair and secretly spy on us. How many dare to jump in front of us?”

These high-level worlds didn’t dare to cause trouble.

If they caused trouble, no Eighth Tribulation Eminence would take action! Eighth Tribulation Eminences had precious time. They didn’t have the time to busy themselves with their descendants. If they dared to go out and cause trouble, their death was deserving.

From an Eighth Tribulation Eminence’s point of view, they would look down at the timeline and see countless juniors in their homeworld across billions of years. Very few people could attract their attention.

Of course, Meng Chuan and Hall Lord White Avian knew that they could only endure being spied on.

“By the way, the phoenixes will likely visit us in the near future,” said Hall Lord White Avian. “I guess they agreed to your request.”

Meng Chuan smiled. “I requested to visit the phoenixes’ library more than 100 years ago and said the price was negotiable. They only decided now? I thought there was no hope.”

Hall Lord White Avian said, “The phoenixes are similar to the dragons. The upper echelons need to discuss it; it’s not something the clan leader can decide on her own. Some of the upper echelons are in seclusion, while others might object. It’s already considered fast to gather the upper echelons and spend about a century to make a decision.”

Meng Chuan felt anticipation when he heard that.

After Hall Lord White Avian informed him of the good news, he left. Meng Chuan continued reading.

After cultivating to Meng Chuan’s level, he naturally had to study more if he wanted to improve his willpower. Therefore, he read books! He read Eighth Tribulation manuals and Seventh Tribulation manuals. Since his targets were at least Seventh Tribulation manuals, White Avian Hall’s library wasn’t enough. Hence, he also requested help from various factions. He was willing to spend a large number of treasures to exchange for them.

He wouldn’t take advantage of others; he would exchange for it at a fair price and read it once. But the more precious the manuals were, the harder it was to find them. Many of them were in the collections of the dragons, phoenixes, and many other high-level worlds. The phoenixes seemed to agree on this matter, so Meng Chuan was rather expectant.

Considering my realm, I can easily learn a Seventh Tribulation ultimate technique. I can also understand many Eighth Tribulation manuals. As Meng Chuan read and cultivated, his understanding of the countless phenomena in the universe deepened, and his willpower slowly improved. He believed that if this continued, he would definitely have a chance of bearing the evolution of the spacetime law in his life.

Mandala Finger is a psyche finger art? It’s really interesting how it affects one’s mind. It’s a unique method. Meng Chuan looked at the ultimate technique in his hand. It was created by a half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminence, and he felt that he had gained quite a lot.

Just as he was in a good mood and was deep in studying this finger art—

Boom!

There was a loud boom!

Meng Chuan put down the book in his hand. He felt as though his Essence Soul World had split apart. With a loud bang, it began to evolve spacetime…

Di Alam Mimpi Surgawi, Pohon Sambungan Surga menghubungkan langit dan tanah. Daun biasa lebarnya puluhan kilometer. Mahkota Pohon Sambungan Surga membentang lebih dari 50.000 kilometer, dan ada bentangan bangunan di atasnya. Di situlah seluruh pengadilan ilahi Alam Mimpi Surgawi berada.

Di aula tersembunyi di pengadilan ilahi Alam Mimpi Surgawi.

Seorang tetua berambut putih duduk bersila. Di sampingnya adalah seorang wanita tua, yang sedang menunggunya dalam diam.

Kekuatan sesepuh berambut putih melonjak menjadi formasi susunan kuno yang tersembunyi di aula. Melalui formasi susunan, gelombang tak terlihat menyebar di Sungai Ruangwaktu.

Biarkan dunia memasuki mimpiku. Kesadaran sesepuh berambut putih memasuki dunia mimpi, dan dunia mimpi mencerminkan seluruh Sungai Ruangwaktu.

Penatua berambut putih melihat ke berbagai tempat. Terlepas dari Menara Abadi, Aula Iblis Hitam, Tanah Leluhur Naga, dunia berpenghuni tingkat tinggi, dan beberapa area di mana Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Eminences telah mengatur formasi array, dia bisa melihat yang lainnya. Bahkan Balai Burung Putih dan tempat lain tidak dapat menghentikannya.

City Lord Eastcalm, Hall Lord White Avian. Penatua berambut putih secara alami menyelidiki dua keberadaan terkuat di River of Spacetime. Di dunia mimpi ilusi, tidak ada makhluk hidup lain yang bisa merasakan mata-matanya. Meng Chuan dan Hall Lord White Avian bisa merasakannya, tetapi sulit bagi mereka untuk mengetahui dari mana mata-mata itu berasal.

Bagaimana dua setengah-langkah Kedelapan Kedelapan Eminences menahan cara nenek moyang?

Nenek moyangnya adalah Pemimpin Kesengsaraan Kedelapan. Dia terampil dalam mimpi dan sangat pandai memata-matai.

Dengan mimpi, dia bisa memimpikan segalanya di River of Spacetime. Itu tidak mungkin untuk dilawan.

Penatua berambut putih adalah dewa Kesengsaraan Ketujuh. Dia menggunakan formasi susunan yang ditinggalkan oleh nenek moyangnya untuk mencerminkan seluruh Sungai Ruangwaktu ke dalam mimpinya.

Suara mendesing.

Penatua berambut putih membuka matanya setelah menghentikan formasi array.

“Yang Mulia,” kata wanita tua itu.

Aku menggunakan formasi susunan nenek moyang untuk mengamati berbagai area di Sungai Ruangwaktu. Dibandingkan dengan 300 tahun lalu, tidak banyak perubahan, ”kata sesepuh berambut putih itu. “Hall Lord White Avian terkuat dan City Lord Eastcalm masih berada di ranah setengah langkah Kesengsaraan Kedelapan saja.”

Meskipun dunia tingkat tinggi hanya memiliki sedikit kontak dengan dunia luar, mereka sering mengamati perubahan di dunia luar.

Wanita tua itu mengangguk sedikit. Dia bukan dewa; dia adalah ahli terkuat di era Alam Mimpi Surgawi saat ini, Eminence Kesengsaraan Keenam. Setelah berkultivasi ke alam seperti itu … saat nenek moyang berikutnya bangun, dia akan dianugerahi kursi ketuhanan ketika dia meninggal. Sejak saat itu, dia akan hidup selama surga.

Penatua berambut putih adalah Dewa Kesengsaraan Ketujuh. Dia adalah yang terkuat dalam sejarah Alam Mimpi Surgawi selain dari Nenek Moyang. Hanya dengan kekuatan Ketujuh Kesengsaraan Eminence dia bisa lebih baik melemparkan banyak formasi array yang ditinggalkan oleh nenek moyang.

Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Eminences harus melewati periode waktu satu demi satu sehingga mereka dapat mempertahankan kemudaan mereka. Dewa-dewa ini sudah lama hidup. Meskipun mereka mengandalkan metode tidur dan reinkarnasi, kesadaran mereka tetap terdistorsi.

Seiring waktu, mereka akan menjadi berbeda. Mereka secara bertahap akan diasimilasi oleh keilahian mereka; ini adalah sesuatu yang tidak dapat membantu. Tanpa kemauan yang cukup, mereka tidak akan mampu mempertahankan diri meskipun memiliki umur yang panjang.

“Ngomong-ngomong, lihat jejak waktu di City Lord Eastcalm,” kata tetua berambut putih sambil tersenyum. “Dia telah berkultivasi selama lebih dari 33.000 tahun. Dibandingkan terakhir kali… City Lord Eastcalm pasti memiliki avatar Essence Soul yang terletak di tempat di mana waktu mengalir 33 kali lebih cepat. ”

Dia adalah Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan setengah langkah. Sebuah tempat yang dapat mempertahankan aliran waktu 33 kali lipat lebih cepat dari biasanya? Seberapa menakutkankah pengeluaran energi? ” Wanita tua itu terkejut. Aku belum pernah mendengar tempat seperti itu.

Karena itu, dia pasti menemukan kesempatan khusus. Dia mungkin telah melampaui alam semesta, “kata sesepuh berambut putih itu.

Itu wsangat normal untuk pergi ke luar alam semesta.

Hall Lord White Avian, Celestial Thearch Myriad Star, dan Guild Master Fiend Eye memiliki peluang serupa di masa lalu. Mereka telah memperoleh dukungan dari Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan. Jika mereka mau mengeluarkannya, mereka secara alami bisa berkeliaran di luar alam semesta.

“Yang Mulia, kapan Kamu berencana memasuki tidur Kamu?” tanya wanita tua itu.

“Di era ini, dua setengah langkah Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Kedelapan masih hidup. Aku tidak akan tidur untuk saat ini; Aku akan tidur setelah mereka meninggal karena usia tua, “kata penatua berambut putih itu.

“Menurut aliran waktu 33 kali lipat lebih cepat, City Lord Eastcalm akan mencapai akhir masa pakainya dalam 5.000 tahun. Kami akan melihat hasil kultivasinya, ”kata wanita tua itu sambil tersenyum.

Dia tidak akan berhasil. Penatua berambut putih itu menggelengkan kepalanya. “Nenek moyang berkata bahwa mencapai alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan sangatlah sulit. Alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Jiwa Inti … bahkan lebih sulit dijangkau daripada alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Tubuh Fisik. ”

Wanita tua itu mengangguk sedikit dan berkata, “Ngomong-ngomong, Yang Mulia, muridku — Meng Hu — pernah berteman baik dengan Tuan Kota Eastcalm. Mereka membersihkan Gunung Fiend bersama-sama. ”

“Bagaimana pengalamannya di Hundred Lives Dream Realm?” tanya orang tua berambut putih. Sebagai Pemimpin Kesengsaraan Kelima di Alam Mimpi Surgawi, Meng Hu juga mendapat perhatian yang sama. Lagipula, itu sudah tidak buruk bagi Yang Mulia Kesengsaraan Keenam muncul di dunia tingkat tinggi. Seringkali, tidak ada Eminences Kesengsaraan Keenam.

“Dia sudah mengalaminya lebih dari setengahnya, tapi dia masih belum tenggelam ke dalamnya,” kata wanita tua itu dengan cemas. “Namun, semakin dalam seseorang memasuki Alam Impian Seratus Kehidupan, semakin berbahaya jadinya.”

“Jika dia selamat, dia akan mendapat manfaat dari bencana. Dia juga akan mencapai alam Kesengsaraan Keenam dalam hidupnya, ”kata sesepuh berambut putih itu. “Jika dia gagal, itu berarti dia tidak memiliki sikap yang diperlukan.”

…

Di alam luar, White Avian Hall, Perpustakaan.

Meng Chuan sedang membaca buku.

Hah? Meng Chuan sedikit mengerutkan kening saat dia samar-samar merasakan seseorang memata-matai dia. Faksi tingkat tinggi mana yang memata-matai Aku?

Setelah Meng Chuan menjadi setengah langkah Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Eminence, dia mengetahui bahwa faksi dari dunia tingkat tinggi kadang-kadang akan memata-matai berbagai tempat di Sungai Ruangwaktu. Sebelum dia memahami hukum ruang waktu, dia tidak menyadarinya. Sekarang dia telah menemukan mereka … dia tidak tahu faksi mana yang memata-matai dia. Lagipula, ada lusinan faksi di River of Spacetime.

Setiap faksi memiliki Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan yang mendukung mereka.

Segera, sensasi mata-mata menghilang.

Meng Chuan. Hall Lord White Avian datang ke perpustakaan.

“Hall Lord, kamu juga merasakannya?” Meng Chuan memandang Hall Lord White Avian.

“Iya.” Hall Lord White Avian mengangguk. “Namun, jangan khawatir tentang itu. Mereka hanya bisa bersembunyi di sarang mereka dan diam-diam memata-matai kita. Berapa banyak yang berani melompat di depan kita? ”

Dunia tingkat tinggi ini tidak berani menimbulkan masalah.

Jika mereka menyebabkan masalah, tidak ada Yang Mulia Kesengsaraan Kedelapan yang akan mengambil tindakan! Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Eminences memiliki waktu yang berharga. Mereka tidak punya waktu untuk menyibukkan diri dengan keturunannya. Jika mereka berani keluar dan menimbulkan masalah, kematian mereka memang pantas.

Dari sudut pandang Eighth Tribulation Eminence, mereka akan melihat garis waktu dan melihat junior yang tak terhitung jumlahnya di dunia asal mereka selama miliaran tahun. Sangat sedikit orang yang bisa menarik perhatian mereka.

Tentu saja, Meng Chuan dan Hall Lord White Avian tahu bahwa mereka hanya tahan dimata-matai.

“Ngomong-ngomong, burung phoenix kemungkinan akan mengunjungi kita dalam waktu dekat,” kata Hall Lord White Avian. “Aku kira mereka menyetujui permintaan Kamu.”

Meng Chuan tersenyum. “Aku meminta untuk mengunjungi perpustakaan burung phoenix lebih dari 100 tahun yang lalu dan mengatakan harganya bisa dinegosiasikan. Mereka baru memutuskan sekarang? Aku pikir tidak ada harapan. ”

Hall Lord White Avian berkata, “Burung phoenix mirip dengan naga. Para eselon atas perlu membahasnya; itu bukan sesuatu yang dapat diputuskan sendiri oleh pemimpin klan. Beberapa eselon atas mengasingkan diri, sementara yang lain mungkin keberatan. Sudah dianggap cepat untuk mengumpulkan eselon atas dan menghabiskan sekitar satu abad untuk membuat keputusan. ”

Meng Chuantisipasi yang terasa ketika dia mendengar itu.

Setelah Hall Lord White Avian memberitahunya tentang kabar baik, dia pergi. Meng Chuan terus membaca.

Setelah berkultivasi ke tingkat Meng Chuan, dia secara alami harus belajar lebih banyak jika dia ingin meningkatkan kemauannya. Karena itu, dia membaca buku! Dia membaca manual Kedelapan Kesengsaraan dan manual Kesengsaraan Ketujuh. Karena targetnya setidaknya manual Kesengsaraan Ketujuh, perpustakaan White Avian Hall tidak cukup. Makanya, dia pun meminta bantuan dari berbagai faksi. Dia rela menghabiskan banyak harta untuk ditukar dengan mereka.

Dia tidak akan memanfaatkan orang lain; dia akan menukarnya dengan harga yang pantas dan membacanya sekali. Tapi semakin berharga manualnya, semakin sulit menemukannya. Banyak dari mereka berada dalam koleksi naga, burung phoenix, dan banyak dunia tingkat tinggi lainnya. Burung-burung phoenix tampaknya menyetujui masalah ini, jadi Meng Chuan agak berharap.

Mempertimbangkan dunia Aku, Aku dapat dengan mudah mempelajari teknik pamungkas Kesengsaraan Ketujuh. Aku juga bisa memahami banyak manual Kesengsaraan Kedelapan. Saat Meng Chuan membaca dan berkultivasi, pemahamannya tentang fenomena yang tak terhitung jumlahnya di alam semesta semakin dalam, dan kemauannya perlahan meningkat. Dia percaya bahwa jika ini terus berlanjut, dia pasti akan memiliki kesempatan untuk membawa evolusi hukum ruangwaktu dalam hidupnya.

Mandala Finger adalah seni jari jiwa? Sangat menarik bagaimana hal itu memengaruhi pikiran seseorang. Itu adalah metode yang unik. Meng Chuan melihat teknik pamungkas di tangannya. Itu diciptakan oleh Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan setengah langkah, dan dia merasa bahwa dia telah memperoleh cukup banyak.

Saat dia sedang dalam suasana hati yang baik dan sedang mempelajari seni jari ini—

Ledakan!

Ada ledakan keras!

Meng Chuan meletakkan buku itu di tangannya. Dia merasa seolah-olah Essence Soul World-nya telah terpecah. Dengan ledakan keras, ruangwaktu mulai berkembang…
