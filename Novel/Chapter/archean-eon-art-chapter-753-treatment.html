Chapter 753: Treatment

Translator: CKtalon 

Meng Chuan looked up and sensed the Heavenly Tribulation. It was targeting him—there was no escaping it.

I’ll have to prepare for the Tribulation Transcendence next. Meng Chuan knew that he had to seize every moment.

Whoosh.

In the library, Meng Chuan placed the books on the bookshelf in front of him. He then stood up and walked out of the library.

A group of Eminences had already gathered outside the library. They were all from White Avian Hall—Hall Lord White Avian, Lord of Shadowfiend, Deputy Hall Lord Azure Dragon, Deputy Hall Lord Blazing Sun, Palace Lord Food God, Lord of Shadows, Lord of the East Abyss, Cang Li, and Mountain Lord Moxie.

When Meng Chuan walked out, all of them looked at Meng Chuan with complicated expressions. They were filled with disbelief, amazement, and puzzlement…

“Meng Chuan, you broke through?” asked Hall Lord White Avian. The other Eminences listened carefully.

Meng Chuan smiled and nodded. “I have, but I still need to transcend the eighth Essence Soul Tribulation.”

The moment that was said, the Eminences present fell silent. He has really broken through! He has reached the legendary Eighth Tribulation realm!

Although Meng Chuan still needed to transcend the tribulation before he could become an extended existence, Meng Chuan had already become an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence from this moment onward. He now had unimaginable strength at that level.

Eighth Tribulation realm—this was a realm every Seventh Tribulation Eminence yearned for! Upon reaching this realm, one would gain many unimaginable means. They could make their homeworld a high-level world, and they could allow a portion of their clansmen to transcend Samsara and share lifespans with their homeworld. They could also explore endless spacetime and see scenery thousands of times more exciting.

Typically, the probability of a Seventh Tribulation Eminence reaching the Eighth Tribulation realm was extremely low. But in this era—where three half-step Eighth Tribulation Eminences had once stood shoulder to shoulder—Meng Chuan had taken the critical step and truly reached the Eighth Tribulation realm. Only the final tribulation trial remained.

“Congratulations, Eastcalm.” Lord of Shadowfiend congratulated him.

“Congratulations, City Lord Eastcalm.” All the Eminences present congratulated him. At that moment, their attitudes were much more humbler.

Seventh Tribulation Eminences could only affect an era after all. The River of Spacetime’s fundamental situation was decided by Eighth Tribulation Eminences. If an Eighth Tribulation Eminence wanted to build a faction, they could exist for billions of years. If they offended an Eighth Tribulation Eminence, even someone as powerful as Celestial Thearch Myriad Star would end up in a tragic state.

“Do you need to keep your breakthrough a secret?” asked Hall Lord White Avian.

“There’s no need to keep it a secret.” Meng Chuan shook his head. He believed that many Eighth Tribulation Eminences in the River of Spacetime had sensed his elevation in life’s natural order.

He could vaguely sense that there were Eighth Tribulation Eminences sleeping and hiding in this universe. However, they were isolated by array formations. Meng Chuan could determine that they were still alive, but he didn’t know their exact locations.

He had just broken through, but without an array formation isolating him, the Eighth Tribulation Eminences would’ve known about his breakthrough. There was no need to hide it.

“Understood.” Hall Lord White Avian nodded before he couldn’t help saying, “Meng Chuan, I have something to discuss with you.”

“Hall Lord, let’s talk in detail when we reach your residence.” Meng Chuan smiled. He naturally guessed what Hall Lord wanted to say.

Soon, the two went to a courtyard in the hall. The other Eminences didn’t dare to disturb them.

“Look at my injuries. Can they be treated?” asked Hall Lord White Avian immediately as he sat down.

Meng Chuan looked at Hall Lord White Avian, but he could already see the other party’s Essence Soul clearly. He saw the alien powers that permeated everywhere.

It was a Power of Essence Soul at the same level as him.

“In terms of bodies, a Physical Body Eighth Tribulation Eminence has the advantage,” said Meng Chuan. “But in terms of unpredictable strength, an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence’s Power of Essence Soul is stronger. The one who attacked you was an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence. The Power of Essence Soul seeped into one of your avatars, passed through karma and your thoughts, and naturally transmitted to your homeworld’s true body.”

“Thought transmission?” Hall Lord White Avian was alarmed.

“By knowing him, remembering him, and understanding him, his strength is naturally able to seep into you,” explained Meng Chuan. “If he’s willing, he can even use your outer realm true body’s mark to condense an Essence Soul true body and descend into our universe. Of course, he didn’t kill you because your homeworld’s true body has always been in your homeworld, and he’s unable to enter your homeworld.”

“Yes, our universe is Dragon Ancestor’s home universe. I heard that he’s quite famous beyond the universe, so he won’t easily storm his way here,” said Hall Lord White Avian self-deprecatingly. “In his eyes, I’m probably a tiny ant that’s not worth mentioning. Just a nap, and I’ll die of old age. It’s not worth paying a huge price for me.”

As Meng Chuan listened, his Power of Essence Soul seeped into Hall Lord White Avian.

Hall Lord White Avian suddenly felt like Meng Chuan’s eyes were like an endless universe. He couldn’t help but be dazed.

Meng Chuan’s Power of Essence Soul affected Hall Lord White Avian’s mind. Through karma and mental transmission, it also seeped into his other true body in his homeworld.

His two true bodies were affected at the same time.

Hall Lord White Avian’s mind was slightly distorted and changed. The evil power began to be expelled.

Meng Chuan could sense that the other party was about the same level as him. As a sourceless water, the other party’s infiltration powers naturally couldn’t withstand his; this was like fighting for territory. A Physical Body Seventh Tribulation Eminence like Hall Lord White Avian was unable to stop Meng Chuan and a peer equal to him from corroding him with their Power of Essence Soul.

Moments later, Meng Chuan’s Power of Essence Soul completely expelled the other party’s before retracting his strength.

Huh? Hall Lord White Avian fell into a daze.

“My injuries?” Hall Lord White Avian was pleasantly surprised to discover that he had completely recovered.

“Why can’t I remember what the Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence that injured me looks like?” Hall Lord White Avian immediately discovered the changes in his body. At his realm, he would immediately discover the changes in his body.

“Only by doing so will it be a complete cure,” explained Meng Chuan. “If you still remember him clearly, he will be able to infiltrate and corrode you again.”

Hall Lord White Avian was secretly alarmed.

The Eighth Tribulation Eminences he came into contact with were all Physical Body Eighth Tribulation Eminences.

The only Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence he had met was an enemy. At that moment, he felt that an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence’s means were much more sinister than those of a Physical Body Eighth Tribulation Eminence. It was impossible to guard against them.

“I know that Black Fiend Hall’s Power of Nightmare is strange, but I feel that an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence’s power is much more terrifying. I can’t even know his name or have intelligence on him.” Hall Lord White Avian sighed.

“When I was at the half-step Eighth Tribulation realm, I once treated Poison Eye and witnessed the Power of Nightmare. That was a formulation comprehended by Black Fiend Progenitor,” said Meng Chuan. “An Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence’s power is unique to those who have cultivated the Essence Soul lineage to the Eighth Tribulation realm. It’s not easy for Physical Body Eighth Tribulation Eminences to possess such means.”

Now that Hall Lord White Avian had recovered from his injuries, he was in a much better mood. “Back then, I thought that if an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence were to appear in this era, only you would stand a chance. However, I was just clinging onto any hope of survival in my despair. I knew how difficult it was to give birth to an Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence. Who knew that you would really succeed?”

Meng Chuan shook his head and said, “I haven’t transcended the tribulation yet.”

“You will definitely succeed.” Hall Lord White Avian was more confident than Meng Chuan after seeing his journey.

“If I fail to transcend the tribulation, please take care of my homeworld,” said Meng Chuan.

Hall Lord White Avian was slightly taken aback before he said solemnly, “I promise with my life that I will watch over your homeworld with all my might. However, I still believe that it’s not my place to watch over a high-level world when you transcend the tribulation successfully.”

“I’ll be counting on your blessings.” Meng Chuan wasn’t confident because he knew too little about the eighth Essence Soul Tribulation.

Huh? Meng Chuan suddenly sensed something and looked up.

A tall man with long, narrow eyes had already arrived outside the door. He looked at Meng Chuan with kindness in his eyes.

Meng Chuan looked at him.

“Greetings, Eastcalm; I’m Crimson Peace.” The tall man walked into the courtyard. Hall Lord White Avian didn’t notice him at all. He was in a completely different spacetime.

The person was none other than Perfected Lord Crimson Peace, an Eighth Tribulation Eminence.

“Greetings, Perfected Lord Crimson Peace.” As Meng Chuan spoke to Hall Lord White Avian, he sent his Essence Soul avatar into this layer of spacetime and stood up to welcome Perfected Lord Crimson Peace.

Bab 753: Perawatan Penerjemah: CKtalon

Meng Chuan mendongak dan merasakan Kesengsaraan Surgawi. Itu menargetkan dia — tidak ada yang bisa menghindarinya.

Aku harus mempersiapkan Transendensi Kesengsaraan berikutnya. Meng Chuan tahu bahwa dia harus memanfaatkan setiap saat.

Suara mendesing.

Di perpustakaan, Meng Chuan meletakkan buku-buku di rak buku di depannya. Dia kemudian berdiri dan keluar dari perpustakaan.

Sekelompok Yang Mulia sudah berkumpul di luar perpustakaan. Mereka semua dari White Avian Hall — Hall Lord White Avian, Lord of Shadowfiend, Deputy Hall Lord Azure Dragon, Deputy Hall Lord Blazing Sun, Palace Lord Food God, Lord of Shadows, Lord of the East Abyss, Cang Li, dan Mountain Lord Keberanian.

Saat Meng Chuan keluar, mereka semua memandang Meng Chuan dengan ekspresi rumit. Mereka dipenuhi dengan ketidakpercayaan, keheranan, dan kebingungan…

“Meng Chuan, kamu menerobos?” tanya Hall Lord White Avian. Yang Mulia lainnya mendengarkan dengan cermat.

Meng Chuan tersenyum dan mengangguk. “Sudah, tapi Aku masih harus melampaui Essence Soul Tribulation kedelapan.”

Saat itu dikatakan, Yang Mulia hadir terdiam. Dia benar-benar telah menerobos! Dia telah mencapai alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan yang legendaris!

Meskipun Meng Chuan masih perlu mengatasi kesengsaraan sebelum dia bisa menjadi eksistensi yang diperpanjang, Meng Chuan sudah menjadi Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Jiwa Essence mulai saat ini dan seterusnya. Dia sekarang memiliki kekuatan yang tak terbayangkan pada level itu.

Alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan — ini adalah alam yang dirindukan setiap Kesengsaraan Ketujuh yang Mulia! Setelah mencapai alam ini, seseorang akan mendapatkan banyak cara yang tak terbayangkan. Mereka dapat menjadikan dunia rumah mereka sebagai dunia tingkat tinggi, dan mereka dapat mengizinkan sebagian dari klan mereka untuk melampaui Samsara dan berbagi rentang hidup dengan dunia asal mereka. Mereka juga bisa menjelajahi ruangwaktu yang tak ada habisnya dan melihat pemandangan ribuan kali lebih menarik.

Biasanya, kemungkinan Eminence Kesengsaraan Ketujuh mencapai alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan sangat rendah. Tapi di era ini — di mana tiga setengah langkah Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Eminences pernah berdiri bahu-membahu — Meng Chuan telah mengambil langkah kritis dan benar-benar mencapai alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan. Hanya pengadilan kesusahan terakhir yang tersisa.

Selamat, Eastcalm. Lord of Shadowfiend memberi selamat padanya.

Selamat, City Lord Eastcalm. Semua Yang Mulia yang hadir memberi selamat padanya. Pada saat itu, sikap mereka jauh lebih rendah hati.

Seventh Tribulation Eminences hanya dapat mempengaruhi suatu era. Situasi fundamental River of Spacetime diputuskan oleh Eighth Tribulation Eminences. Jika Pemimpin Kesengsaraan Kedelapan ingin membangun faksi, mereka bisa bertahan selama milyaran tahun. Jika mereka menyinggung Yang Mulia Kesengsaraan Kedelapan, bahkan seseorang yang sekuat Bintang Segudang Pencarian Surgawi akan berakhir dalam keadaan tragis.

“Apakah Kamu perlu merahasiakan terobosan Kamu?” tanya Hall Lord White Avian.

Tidak perlu merahasiakannya. Meng Chuan menggelengkan kepalanya. Dia percaya bahwa banyak Pemimpin Kesengsaraan Kedelapan di Sungai Ruang Waktu telah merasakan kenaikannya dalam tatanan alam kehidupan.

Dia samar-samar bisa merasakan bahwa ada Delapan Kesengsaraan Eminences tidur dan bersembunyi di alam semesta ini. Namun, mereka diisolasi oleh formasi array. Meng Chuan dapat memastikan bahwa mereka masih hidup, tetapi dia tidak tahu lokasi persis mereka.

Dia baru saja menerobos, tetapi tanpa formasi array yang mengisolasi dia, Yang Mulia Kesengsaraan Kedelapan akan tahu tentang terobosannya. Tidak perlu menyembunyikannya.

“Dimengerti.” Hall Lord White Avian mengangguk sebelum dia tidak dapat menahan diri untuk tidak berkata, “Meng Chuan, ada sesuatu yang ingin Aku diskusikan dengan Kamu.”

Hall Lord, mari kita bicara secara detail saat kita sampai di kediaman Kamu. Meng Chuan tersenyum. Dia secara alami menebak apa yang ingin dikatakan Hall Lord.

Segera, keduanya pergi ke halaman di aula. Yang Mulia lainnya tidak berani mengganggu mereka.

“Lihat lukaku. Bisakah mereka dirawat? ” tanya Hall Lord White Avian segera saat dia duduk.

Meng Chuan memandang Hall Lord White Avian, tapi dia sudah bisa melihat Essence Soul pihak lain dengan jelas. Dia melihat kekuatan alien yang menyebar di mana-mana.

Itu adalah Kekuatan Jiwa Esensi di level yang sama dengannya.

“Dalam hal tubuh, Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Tubuh Fisik memiliki keuntungan,” kata Meng Chuan. “Tapi dalam hal kekuatan tak terduga, Emi Kesengsaraan Jiwa Kedelapan EssenceKekuatan Essence Soul nence lebih kuat. Orang yang menyerang Kamu adalah Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence. The Power of Essence Soul meresap ke dalam salah satu avatar Kamu, melewati karma dan pikiran Kamu, dan secara alami ditransmisikan ke tubuh sejati dunia asal Kamu. ”

Transmisi pikiran? Hall Lord White Avian terkejut.

“Dengan mengenalnya, mengingatnya, dan memahaminya, kekuatannya secara alami dapat meresap ke dalam diri Kamu,” jelas Meng Chuan. “Jika dia mau, dia bahkan dapat menggunakan tanda tubuh sejati alam luar Kamu untuk memadatkan tubuh sejati Jiwa Esensi dan turun ke alam semesta kita. Tentu saja, dia tidak membunuhmu karena tubuh asli duniamu selalu ada di duniamu, dan dia tidak bisa memasuki duniamu. ”

“Ya, alam semesta kita adalah alam semesta asal Leluhur Naga. Aku mendengar bahwa dia cukup terkenal di luar alam semesta, jadi dia tidak akan dengan mudah menyerbu ke sini, “kata Hall Lord White Avian mencela diri sendiri. “Di matanya, Aku mungkin seekor semut kecil yang tidak layak disebut. Hanya tidur siang, dan aku akan mati karena usia tua. Tidak ada gunanya membayar harga yang mahal untuk Aku. ”

Saat Meng Chuan mendengarkan, Power of Essence Soul-nya meresap ke Hall Lord White Avian.

Hall Lord White Avian tiba-tiba merasa mata Meng Chuan seperti alam semesta tanpa akhir. Dia tidak bisa membantu tetapi linglung.

Kekuatan Jiwa Esensi Meng Chuan memengaruhi pikiran Hall Lord White Avian. Melalui karma dan transmisi mental, itu juga meresap ke dalam tubuh aslinya yang lain di dunia asalnya.

Dua tubuh aslinya terpengaruh pada saat bersamaan.

Pikiran Hall Lord White Avian sedikit terdistorsi dan berubah. Kekuatan jahat mulai dikeluarkan.

Meng Chuan dapat merasakan bahwa pihak lain memiliki level yang sama dengannya. Sebagai air yang tak ternilai, kekuatan infiltrasi pihak lain secara alami tidak dapat menahannya; ini seperti memperebutkan wilayah. Tubuh Fisik Ketujuh Kesengsaraan Yang Mulia seperti Hall Lord White Avian tidak dapat menghentikan Meng Chuan dan rekan yang setara dengannya untuk merusaknya dengan Kekuatan Jiwa Esensi mereka.

Beberapa saat kemudian, Kekuatan Jiwa Esensi Meng Chuan benar-benar mengusir pihak lain sebelum menarik kembali kekuatannya.

Hah? Hall Lord White Avian menjadi linglung.

Luka Aku? Hall Lord White Avian terkejut saat mengetahui bahwa dia telah pulih sepenuhnya.

“Mengapa Aku tidak dapat mengingat seperti apa Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence yang melukai Aku?” Hall Lord White Avian segera menemukan perubahan pada tubuhnya. Di alamnya, dia akan segera menemukan perubahan dalam tubuhnya.

“Hanya dengan melakukan itu maka akan sembuh total,” jelas Meng Chuan. “Jika kamu masih mengingatnya dengan jelas, dia akan dapat menyusup dan membuatmu korosi lagi.”

Hall Lord White Avian diam-diam terkejut.

Para Eminences Kesengsaraan Kedelapan yang berhubungan dengannya adalah semua Eminences Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Tubuh Fisik.

Satu-satunya Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence yang dia temui adalah musuh. Pada saat itu, dia merasa bahwa cara Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Jiwa Essence jauh lebih jahat daripada yang dari Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Tubuh Fisik. Tidak mungkin untuk melindungi mereka.

Aku tahu bahwa Kekuatan Mimpi Buruk Black Fiend Hall itu aneh, tapi aku merasa bahwa kekuatan Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence jauh lebih menakutkan. Aku bahkan tidak tahu namanya atau memiliki kecerdasan padanya. ” Hall Lord White Avian mendesah.

“Ketika Aku berada di alam setengah-langkah Kesengsaraan Kedelapan, Aku pernah merawat Poison Eye dan menyaksikan Kekuatan Mimpi Buruk. Itu adalah rumusan yang dipahami oleh Black Fiend Progenitor, ”kata Meng Chuan. “Jiwa Esensi Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Kekuatan Yang Mulia unik bagi mereka yang telah mengembangkan garis Jiwa Essence ke alam Kesengsaraan Kedelapan. Tidak mudah bagi Tubuh Fisik Kedelapan Kesengsaraan Eminences untuk memiliki sarana seperti itu. ”

Sekarang Hall Lord White Avian telah pulih dari luka-lukanya, suasana hatinya jauh lebih baik. “Saat itu, aku berpikir bahwa jika Eminence Kesengsaraan Kedelapan Jiwa Essence muncul di era ini, hanya kamu yang akan memiliki kesempatan. Namun, Aku hanya berpegang teguh pada harapan untuk bertahan hidup dalam keputusasaan Aku. Aku tahu betapa sulitnya melahirkan Essence Soul Eighth Tribulation Eminence. Siapa yang tahu bahwa Kamu benar-benar akan berhasil? ”

Meng Chuan menggelengkan kepalanya dan berkata, “Aku belum melampaui tribulation lagi. ”

“Kamu pasti akan berhasil.” Hall Lord White Avian lebih percaya diri daripada Meng Chuan setelah melihat perjalanannya.

“Jika Aku gagal mengatasi kesengsaraan, mohon jaga dunia rumah Aku,” kata Meng Chuan.

Hall Lord White Avian sedikit terkejut sebelum dia berkata dengan sungguh-sungguh, “Aku berjanji dengan hidup Aku bahwa Aku akan menjaga dunia rumah Kamu dengan sekuat tenaga. Namun, Aku masih percaya bahwa ini bukan tempat Aku untuk mengawasi dunia tingkat tinggi ketika Kamu berhasil mengatasi kesengsaraan. ”

Aku akan mengandalkan berkat Kamu. Meng Chuan tidak percaya diri karena dia tahu terlalu sedikit tentang Essence Soul Tribulation kedelapan.

Hah? Meng Chuan tiba-tiba merasakan sesuatu dan mendongak.

Seorang pria jangkung dengan mata panjang dan sipit telah tiba di luar pintu. Dia menatap Meng Chuan dengan kebaikan di matanya.

Meng Chuan menatapnya.

“Salam, Eastcalm; Aku adalah Crimson Peace. ” Pria jangkung itu berjalan ke halaman. Hall Lord White Avian sama sekali tidak memperhatikannya. Dia berada di ruangwaktu yang sama sekali berbeda.

Orang itu tidak lain adalah Perfected Lord Crimson Peace, seorang Eighth Tribulation Eminence.

Salam, Perfect Lord Crimson Peace. Saat Meng Chuan berbicara dengan Hall Lord White Avian, dia mengirim avatar Essence Soul-nya ke lapisan ruangwaktu ini dan berdiri untuk menyambut Perfected Lord Crimson Peace.
