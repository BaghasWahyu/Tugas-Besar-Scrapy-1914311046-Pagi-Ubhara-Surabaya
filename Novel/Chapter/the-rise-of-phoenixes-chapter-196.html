Chapter 196: Chapter 196

Gu Nanyi suddenly said, “Diarrhea.”

Ning Cheng paused in understanding. Feng Zhiwei had eaten all that raw seafood and drank so much the night before, ending in puking and diarrhea and unrestful sleep. Then she had headed to that battle wits with Zhou Xizhong before rushing anxiously her to the Ancestral Temple. She had pushed her body and mind to the limits, and her weakened body had not been able to resist the illness.

Ning Yi bit his lips, his face as pale as frosted autumn grass. Feng Zhiwei’s body was a feverish stove in his arms, and she had clearly been ill for a while now. When had it begun? She had refused to say a word, not allowing herself to collapse until everything had settled!

She must have known she was infected a long time ago, and so she had kept him distant, and he had thought…

Ning Yi knelt in the dust, paying no mind to his dirty robes. He hugged Feng Zhiwei close, his hands trembling.

He had never hated his own blindness so much!

Gu Nanyi stood numbly beside them, walnuts in his hand. He stared blankly at the dark green climbing over Feng Zhiwei’s face… She was sick? When? How? Why did he not know?

And that Ning Yi, why was his expression so dark? Was she going to die?

Would she die?

As soon as the question appeared in his mind, Gu Nanyi trembled.

He felt a dark, uncomfortable feeling rise up within him as if his chest was plugged. His breath came raggedly as he experienced a strange emotion for the first time.

His life had always been a lake of dead water, his heart always beating to the same rhythm. Sadness, pain, happiness, joy… everything that a normal person had as missing from his life, and he did not understand any of it.

When he had lost his father at age three, he had been very calm.

When his wet-nurse and nanny had died when he was eight, she had taken his hand, her eyes teary as she said: “Poor child, for a person like you to bear so much…”

As he stared indifferently at his wet-nurse that night, he had calmly taken back his hands and wiped off her tears.

Then he had turned and walked through a house filled with bowing attendants.

What was he? What? No one had ever told him, and everyone looked at him that way with those strange eyes, sighing as they walked by him.

He cared nothing for what had happened or those eyes or expressions. His own life seemed like the life of a stranger, distant as if coming to him from a wide expanse of mountain and sea, as if from a different world.

But in this moment he suddenly wished to know what kind of person he was.

Was it because he was different that though he was always by Feng Zhiwei’s side, he still could not understand what had happened to her?

And if she died… if she died…

Gu Nanyi stepped back, frowning and he touched his chest, shutting his eyes and taking deep breaths… He must have been infected as well, that was why he was in pain.

Feng Zhiwei turned her head and began vomiting. She had not eaten much, so she could only spew out gastric juice and bile. Her vomit shot out violently like liquid arrows, covering Ning Yi and not sparing Ning Cheng or Gu Nanyi.

But none of them moved away, and even Gu Nanyi’s obsessive cleanliness did not make him step away.

Ning Yi clutched her even tighter, propping her up on his knee and gently patting her back, relieving the pressure from her internal organs and helping her breath after the violent vomiting, paying no mind to the filth or smell.

Suddenly, the distant sounds of marching filled the sky and a great sea appeared in view. Feng Zhou’s troops had finally arrived.

Ning Yi turned his head, his cold stare locked onto the ajar gates of the Yan Family’s Ancestral Temple, his deep calm eyes revealing an unquenchable fury.

“Tear down the temple!”

“Your Highness!”

“Kill anyone who stands in your way!”

…

A shroud of misery and darkness reigned over West Yard.

The Imperial Envoy had been infected by the plague and his life was in great peril. News of this matter was sealed, and everyone in the know had been forbidden from speaking even to their own men. Prince Chu’s fury was like a great storm, and people walked hurriedly here and there in silent trepidation. No one dared speak, and whenever people met in the hallways and streets, they exchanged terrified glanced before hurrying off on their missions to find physicians and medicine.

A series of physicians were ushered in one after the other as piles of rare, expensive medicine were gathered like worthless trash. The medicine stoves were cooking day and night, and Prince Chu’s face great more and more pale as the days passed.

After that day’s great fury, he had not spoken a single word to those around him. He could not stay by Feng Zhiwei’s sickbed, sending off a secret missive for the Imperial Court’s Grand Physicians before spending the days in meetings, interrogating the spies that they had captured.

Feng Zhiwei had fallen to the plague and struggled on the edge of life and death. The South Sea was plunged into upheaval as she lingered in a coma.

An enraged Ning Yi finally bared his cold and merciless fangs.

After Hua Qiong had helped Yan Huaishi and his invalid mother limp out of the gates, Ning Yi had sealed the gates once more, locking the Yan Family inside.

While the rest of the extended family was off receiving their free rations and silver, Ning Yi had the three thousand Imperial Guards and three thousand Feng Zhou troops dig a tunnel underneath the Ancestral Temple before stashing a huge amount of dynamite right below the structure. With a muffled boom the storied walls of South Sea’s wealthiest family’s Ancestral Temple, said to be built by an ancient Emperor, collapsed. The magnificent building, the gorgeously carved beams and painted pillars, the site of worship for generations over the centuries, shattered into broken ruins, revealing the pillars to the golden sun.

Basically every single important male descendant of the family was inside the temple, and fortunately the foundations of the building were firm. Though the building had collapsed, the pillars within held firm. The devastating blow of extinction never came, but one man still died and many were injured. The current Family Head hit his head and fell into a coma; Yan Huaiyuan’s legs were broken by a falling wall. Grandfather Yan was not harmed in anyway, but when the younger generation tried to carry him away and flee, the old man pushed them away and fell into a fit of tears.

He knelt before the broken spirit tablet of the Yan Family’s founding Emperor and kowtowed until his forehead bled, exclaiming: “The heavens have forsaken our Yan Family! Dewei[1] has brought shame to the ancestors and cannot face the ancestors!”

That day, he committed suicide by smashing his head into a wall of the Ancestral Temple, his blood quietly pooling around him in the flying dragon patterns on the ground.

[1] Yan Dewei is Grandfather Yan’s name

Bab 196: Bab 196 Gu Nanyi tiba-tiba berkata, “Diare.”

Ning Cheng berhenti sejenak untuk memahami. Feng Zhiwei telah makan semua makanan laut mentah itu dan minum begitu banyak pada malam sebelumnya, berakhir dengan muntah dan diare serta tidur yang tidak nyenyak. Kemudian dia menuju ke kecerdasan pertempuran itu dengan Zhou Xizhong sebelum bergegas dengan cemas ke Kuil Leluhur. Dia telah mendorong tubuh dan pikirannya ke batas, dan tubuhnya yang lemah tidak mampu menahan penyakit.

Ning Yi menggigit bibirnya, wajahnya sepucat rumput musim gugur yang beku. Tubuh Feng Zhiwei seperti kompor demam dalam pelukannya, dan dia jelas sakit untuk sementara waktu sekarang. Kapan itu dimulai? Dia menolak untuk mengatakan sepatah kata pun, tidak membiarkan dirinya runtuh sampai semuanya beres!

Dia pasti tahu dia terinfeksi sejak lama, jadi dia menjauhkannya, dan dia berpikir…

Ning Yi berlutut di atas debu, tidak menghiraukan jubahnya yang kotor. Dia memeluk Feng Zhiwei erat-erat, tangannya gemetar.

Dia tidak pernah begitu membenci kebutaannya sendiri!

Gu Nanyi berdiri kaku di samping mereka, kenari di tangannya. Dia menatap kosong ke arah hijau tua yang memanjat wajah Feng Zhiwei… Dia sakit? Kapan? Bagaimana? Kenapa dia tidak tahu?

Dan Ning Yi itu, mengapa ekspresinya begitu gelap? Apakah dia akan mati?

Apakah dia akan mati?

Begitu pertanyaan muncul di benaknya, Gu Nanyi gemetar.

Dia merasakan perasaan gelap dan tidak nyaman muncul di dalam dirinya seolah-olah dadanya tersumbat. Napasnya tersengal-sengal saat dia mengalami emosi aneh untuk pertama kalinya.

Hidupnya selalu menjadi danau air mati, jantungnya selalu berdetak dengan ritme yang sama. Kesedihan, rasa sakit, kebahagiaan, kegembiraan… segala sesuatu yang dimiliki orang normal yang hilang dari hidupnya, dan dia tidak memahaminya.

Ketika dia kehilangan ayahnya pada usia tiga tahun, dia sangat tenang.

Ketika pengasuh dan pengasuhnya meninggal ketika dia berusia delapan tahun, dia memegang tangannya, matanya berkaca-kaca saat dia berkata: “Kasihan anak, untuk orang sepertimu menanggung begitu banyak …”

Saat dia menatap acuh tak acuh pada perawat basahnya malam itu, dia dengan tenang menarik kembali tangannya dan menyeka air matanya.

Kemudian dia berbalik dan berjalan melewati sebuah rumah yang penuh dengan pelayan yang membungkuk.

Apa dia? Apa? Tidak ada yang pernah memberitahunya, dan semua orang memandangnya seperti itu dengan mata aneh itu, mendesah saat mereka berjalan di dekatnya.

Dia tidak peduli dengan apa yang telah terjadi atau mata atau ekspresi itu. Hidupnya sendiri tampak seperti kehidupan orang asing, jauh seolah-olah datang kepadanya dari hamparan gunung dan laut yang luas, seolah-olah dari dunia yang berbeda.

Tetapi pada saat ini dia tiba-tiba ingin tahu orang seperti apa dia.

Apakah karena dia berbeda sehingga meskipun dia selalu berada di sisi Feng Zhiwei, dia masih tidak mengerti apa yang telah terjadi padanya?

Dan jika dia meninggal … jika dia meninggal …

Gu Nanyi melangkah mundur, mengerutkan kening dan dia menyentuh dadanya, menutup matanya dan menarik napas dalam-dalam… Dia pasti telah terinfeksi juga, itulah mengapa dia kesakitan.

Feng Zhiwei menoleh dan mulai muntah. Dia belum makan banyak, jadi dia hanya bisa memuntahkan jus lambung dan empedu. Muntahannya keluar dengan keras seperti anak panah cair, menutupi Ning Yi dan tidak menyisakan Ning Cheng atau Gu Nanyi.

Tetapi tidak ada dari mereka yang pindah, dan bahkan kebersihan obsesif Gu Nanyi tidak membuatnya menjauh.

Ning Yi mencengkeramnya lebih erat lagi, menopangnya di atas lututnya dan dengan lembut menepuk punggungnya, menghilangkan tekanan dari organ dalamnya dan membantunya bernapas setelah muntah yang hebat, tidak memedulikan kotoran atau baunya.

Tiba-tiba, suara pawai di kejauhan memenuhi langit dan laut besar muncul. Pasukan Feng Zhou akhirnya tiba.

Ning Yi menoleh, tatapan dinginnya terkunci ke gerbang terbuka Kuil Leluhur Keluarga Yan, matanya yang dalam dan tenang mengungkapkan amarah yang tak terpadamkan.

“Hancurkan kuil!”

“Yang mulia!”

“Bunuh siapa saja yang menghalangi jalanmu!”

…

Selubung kesengsaraan dan kegelapan menguasai West Yard.

Utusan Kekaisaran telah terinfeksi oleh wabah dan hidupnya dalam bahaya besar. Berita tentang masalah ini telah disegel, dan semua orang yang tahu telah dilarang berbicara bahkan kepada orang-orang mereka sendiri. Kemarahan Pangeran Chu seperti badai besar, dan orang-orang berjalan tergesa-gesa kesana-kemari dalam kegelisahan. Tidak ada yang berani berbicara, dan setiap kali orang bertemu di thDi lorong dan jalan, mereka saling bertukar pandangan ketakutan sebelum bergegas menjalankan misi mereka untuk menemukan dokter dan obat-obatan.

Serangkaian dokter diantar satu demi satu saat tumpukan obat langka dan mahal dikumpulkan seperti sampah yang tidak berharga. Kompor obat memasak siang dan malam, dan wajah Pangeran Chu semakin pucat seiring berlalunya waktu.

Setelah kemarahan besar hari itu, dia tidak berbicara sepatah kata pun kepada orang-orang di sekitarnya. Dia tidak bisa tinggal di samping ranjang sakit Feng Zhiwei, mengirimkan surat rahasia untuk Dokter Agung Istana Kerajaan sebelum menghabiskan hari-hari dalam rapat, menginterogasi mata-mata yang mereka tangkap.

Feng Zhiwei telah jatuh ke wabah dan berjuang di ambang hidup dan mati. Laut Selatan terjun ke dalam pergolakan saat dia berada dalam keadaan koma.

Ning Yi yang marah akhirnya memamerkan taringnya yang dingin dan tanpa ampun.

Setelah Hua Qiong membantu Yan Huaishi dan ibunya yang cacat keluar dari gerbang, Ning Yi menyegel gerbang sekali lagi, mengunci Keluarga Yan di dalam.

Sementara keluarga besar lainnya tidak menerima jatah dan perak gratis mereka, Ning Yi meminta tiga ribu Pengawal Kekaisaran dan tiga ribu pasukan Feng Zhou menggali terowongan di bawah Kuil Leluhur sebelum menyembunyikan sejumlah besar dinamit tepat di bawah struktur. Dengan ledakan yang teredam, dinding bertingkat Kuil Leluhur keluarga terkaya di Laut Selatan, yang konon dibangun oleh Kaisar kuno, runtuh. Bangunan megah, balok yang diukir dengan indah dan pilar yang dicat, tempat pemujaan selama beberapa generasi selama berabad-abad, hancur menjadi reruntuhan yang rusak, menampakkan pilar ke matahari keemasan.

Pada dasarnya setiap keturunan laki-laki penting dari keluarga itu ada di dalam kuil, dan untungnya fondasi bangunan itu kokoh. Meskipun bangunan itu telah runtuh, pilar-pilar di dalamnya tetap kokoh. Pukulan yang menghancurkan dari kepunahan tidak pernah datang, tetapi satu orang masih meninggal dan banyak yang terluka. Kepala Keluarga saat ini membenturkan kepalanya dan jatuh koma; Kaki Yan Huaiyuan patah karena dinding yang jatuh. Kakek Yan tidak terluka sama sekali, tetapi ketika generasi yang lebih muda mencoba membawanya pergi dan melarikan diri, lelaki tua itu mendorong mereka dan jatuh ke dalam air mata.

Dia berlutut di depan tablet roh yang rusak dari Kaisar pendiri Keluarga Yan dan bersujud sampai dahinya berdarah, berseru: “Langit telah meninggalkan Keluarga Yan kita! Dewei [1] telah membuat malu leluhur dan tidak bisa menghadapi leluhur! ”

Hari itu, dia bunuh diri dengan membenturkan kepalanya ke dinding Kuil Leluhur, darahnya diam-diam menggenang di sekitarnya dalam pola naga terbang di tanah.

[1] Yan Dewei adalah nama Kakek Yan
